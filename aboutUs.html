About TCI

Twin Cities Inn have been serving the Minneapolis / Saint Paul area for over 20 years now and it’s our mission to provide our customers with clean, safe, and affordable hotel services. We believe in making everyone feel welcome and valued every time you stay with us. At Twin Cities Inn we pride ourselves on providing the best and highest quality services.

With our specialty rooms and our pet friendliness policy we are among one of the first hotel to ever provide these kind of rooms and services. Specialty room will give individual a more personalized and luxurious experience. With our pet friendliness policy you will have the choice to bring your companion with you every time you travel to the Minneapolis / Saint Paul.



Vision

“Our vision is to create a honestly priced, hotel experience in the Minneapolis / Saint Paul area. At Twin Cities Inn our duty is to bring you the best possible service for a variety of lifestyles at a fair price. All while rewarding our loyal customers every time they stay with us.”
